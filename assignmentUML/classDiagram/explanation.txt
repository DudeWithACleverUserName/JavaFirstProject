Class Diagram of an Order Management System

classes => Customer, Order, NormalOrder, SpecialOrder 

Explanation:

-> Customer class has two attributes(name, location) and two methods for sending and receiving order.
-> Order class also has two attributes(date, number) and two methods for confirming and closing the order.
-> Order class is the superclass of the sub classes SpecialOrder and NormalOrder.
-> Also the subclasses has extra methods to perform specific tasks such as dispatch or receive. 
-> Customer and Order classes are connected with a 1..n relationship that is one customer can order n orders(n being a variable).
